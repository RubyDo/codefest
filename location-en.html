Location

University of Ottawa, in Ottawa, Ontario
75 Laurier Ave E, Ottawa, ON K1N 6N5

Activities will take place in Morisset building (65 MRT) and the University Centre Jock-Turcot (85 UCU).

We encourage participants to walk or take public transit to get there. Parking is also available.

Parking

Parking are from 16.00$ to 22.00$ per day. Their is plenty of parking lot space accross the University of Ottawa campus. The closest to the Morisset/Jock-Turcot building is parking K. Please see the map of the CodeFest area for more informations about available parking lot.

Visit the University of Ottawa Parking services page for more information.

Places to get food

Food and drinks are not provided. So bring your lunch and something to drink. Water fountains are available on campus.

You can also purchase food and drinks on campus:

  • Cafeteria on the second floor of the University Center
  • Second Cup in Morisset building first floor
