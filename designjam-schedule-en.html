Web Experience Toolkit CodeFest 2015, Together for a better Web (Go back to home page).
February 18th and 19th, 2016 University of Ottawa, Ottawa, Ontario
Registration (open soon)

The Schedule (subject to change)

CodeFest 2015’s Design Jam will take place both afternoons of CodeFest. Both afternoons will be Jam packed with information, opportunities to learn, the meeting of new people and the sharing of great ideas.

Please plan to attend the Design Jam for both days. If you can’t make it for both days, please let us know as soon as possible at ws-nw@tbs-sct.gc.ca.

The days of Design Jam will be broken down as follows:

Day one (Thursday)

1:00-1:15pm: Welcome

1:15-1:30pm: Client presentation of the problem, organizational context and users

1:30-2:00pm: Clarifications & questions

2:00-3:00pm: Brainstorming

3:00-3:15pm: Presentation about rapid prototyping

3:15-5:00pm: Prototyping

5:00pm: End of day 1. People can stay longer if they wish

Day two (Friday)

12:00-1:00pm: Doors open

1:00-1:15pm: Review of the afternoon ahead

1:15-3:00pm: Continued prototyping and validation

3:00-3:30pm: Time to prepare the presentation

3:30-5:00pm: Presentations, feedback, deliberation. Prizes!

5:00pm: End of day 2. Let’s go to Mike’s Place!

If you have any questions or concerns, contact us any time at ws-nw@tbs-sct.gc.ca or on Twitter @WebExpToolkit.
